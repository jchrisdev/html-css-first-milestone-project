About Us

247 Playground is a members only basketball court located in Sweden, Gothenburg.
We have professional coaches which will help you to develop on an individual level.
All our members have the opportunity to book appointments 24/7 and soak in all the benefits of a Signature Membership.
Whether it's a summer camp held by a professional athlete or a weekend league game, our programming treats you like a star player.

Why be a member and play with us?

Have Fun!

Whether they are shooting hoops, hanging out with your friends on the bench or making great plays, meeting new people, you will have a fun time joining us.
Basketball is a great way to stay in shape and have fun at the same time.

Lowers Stress

Engaging in physical activity releases endorphins, the feel-good, or happiness, hormones. Playing basketball will also hone your concentration skills and promote mindfulness, which can be effective tools for dealing with anxiety in other areas of your life.

Improves balance and coordination

Basketball requires hand-eye and foot coordination as you maintain your balance throughout movements. As you play, jump, pivot or change direction rapidly with your body.
